Banana and Strawberry Overnight Oats

Do you like bananas? Do you like strawberry? Do you like oats? Then you're gonna love this fast, cheap and easy recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 100 gr Banana (or 1 banana) ---> 89 kcal
  • 60 gr Oatmeal ---> 224 kcal
  • 15 gr Chia Seed ---> 71 kcal
  • 250 ml Almond Milk ---> 35 kcal
  • 100 gr Strawberry ---> 39 kcal
  • 3 gr Stevia ---> 12 kcal
    Optional:
  • 10 gr Cocoa Powder ---> 36 kcal
    Total:
  • Regular Version: 470
  • Cocoa Version: 506

Steps:

  1. In a container, smash the banana.
  2. Put everything else.
  3. Mix, mix, mix
  4. Let it rest inside the fridge for at least one hour.
  5. Enjoy, joy, joy.
