Carrot Cake Overnight Oats

Having a hard time eating your vegetables? With this recipe you're gonna start loving carrots.

Ingredients:

  • 100 gr Carrots (2 small or 1 big) ---> 41 kcal
  • 60 gr Oatmeal ---> 224 kcal
  • 15 gr Chia Seed ---> 71 kcal
  • 250 ml Almond Milk ---> 35 kcal
  • 25 gr Raisins ---> 80 kcal
  • 10 gr Coconut Flakes ---> 64 kcal
  • 4 gr Ground Cinammon ---> 10kcal
  • 4 gr Stevia ---> 16 kcal
    Optional:
  • 10 gr Cocoa Powder ---> 36 kcal
    Total:
  • Regular Version: 541
  • Cocoa Version: 577

Steps:

  1. Grate the carrots.
  2. Put the carrots and all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Mix, mix, mix
  4. Let it rest inside the fridge for at least one hour.
  5. Enjoy, joy, joy.
